Tools for Group Planning

Planning group travel doesn't have to be a pain. Here's how we can help.

Guest Management

With group travel, it's easy to forget who's dropped out or added in. Instagator helps you keep track of group participants and helps you make sure that everyone's on the same page.

Group Activity Input

You shouldn't have to guess how the group feels about activities. Instagator makes it easy for you to get group input on activities to figure out which ones will be the biggest hits.

Document and Payment Tracking

Travel can be expensive and complicated, and it doesn't help that people are forgetful! Instagator helps everyone stay on top of deadlines and documents, and helps you get paid on time.
