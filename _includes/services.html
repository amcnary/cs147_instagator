Tools for Group Planning

Instagator facilitates group travel in two primary ways: first, by helping travel planners manage logistics and get feedback during the planning process; and second, by helping trip participants feel more involved in the planning process and therefore invested in the outcome of the trip.

Guest Management

Instagator helps keep track of group participants and helps make sure that everyone is up to date on trip details.

Group Activity Input

Instagator makes it easy for planners and participants to communicate over activity preferences so that the optimal itinerary can be created.

Task Tracking

Instagator helps trip participants stay on top of deadlines and other to-dos, helping take some stress off of the trip planner.
